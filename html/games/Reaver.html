    About

    Technical Artist

    Created pipeline documents for artist's and level designers, material set-ups, as well as the VFX and sound implementation for the game. I set up our project for a data driven workflow where designers could simply open a csv to edit all of the weapon stats. I also set up a parameterized laser grid system where the designers could easily change the thickness, offset, and length of a laser grid.

    Programmer

    I used blueprints to create the prototype showcase for all of our mechanics aside from ai, and during later development after our team grew in size and converted to a c++ project, i handled the finisher system, back and front end of the UI, dialogue, level streaming, and checkpoint systems, As well as other gameplay systems such as doors and elevators.

    Timeline

    Total 16 weeks

    Reaver started with a team of 3 including myself, We had one week to get a pitch ready, and then over the next few weeks more team members were added as other projects were voted off until week eight when we went into full production with no further additions to the team.
