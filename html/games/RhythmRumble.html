    About

    Lead Programmer

    ·
       it was my responsibility to take on the core mechanics of our game. I wrote a script that would take an image and output notes to the screen, this allowed easily modifiable changes for note positions and song additions. Other tasks included things such as character selection, attacking, and damage scaling.

    Technical Artist

    ·
       Rigging/Animation For our animation's, we had the privilege of working with FIEA. The raw animation data was recorded at their studio with our producer as the actor. The Data was then sent to me after being cleaned up in Axiom's Blade. Using Autodesk Motion Builder, I re-centered all animations to start at origin, then I re-targeted the data onto the models I rigged. The characters faces were skinned with the model but are controlled through a texture sheet animation script i wrote. I created the first character Pat, which served as a base for the character style that the other artists would conform too. I also created shaders used in this project, one of which is on everything aside from UI/HUD, this is basically just a standard ramp shader but with a custom intensity slider for how much light affects it, and a toggle that allows the vertices to pulse. Another is on certain menu items, this shader is a transparent shader that also allows pulsing. The main menu is an example of a uv scrolling shader that I wrote.
