    About

    When I came across this video by Sebastian Lague I felt compelled to have a hand at procedurally animating a spider myself in UE4.
    I wanted to preserve the continuity and organic qualities of the spiders legs so I chose to implement them as splines with meshes deformed around them.
    More in depth Information on my implementation can be read via the video demonstrations above.
